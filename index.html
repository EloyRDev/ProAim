WHAT'S THIS?

PROðŸ—²AIM is a top-down 2D shooting game made in pure JS. No permissions needed, no flash, nothing but a pure web game. You'll have to fight different types of ghosts, which can go through walls (But you can't hehe). Their respawn time will increase over time, and also other types of enemies will appear. But, don't worry, new guns will appear on the map, which could have more range, damage, ammo, or fire speed. Have fun!

WHICH IS THE MAIN OBJECTIVE?

Don't die. Just that. This game has a very fast curve of difficulty. You will start "easy" but in a minute you'll be praying for your life. The main objective is to achieve more points than your friends, just that. Oh and also, having fun! :)

BEFORE STARTING

Keep in mind that, once you press play, the game will start. No pause, no breaks, just keep your attention to the game and reach the maximum points. If you use a laptop or screen smaller than 1920x1080 you may see game window too big, or too zoomed. Just zoom out a little until you se the full map.
